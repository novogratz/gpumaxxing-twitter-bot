## Writing Style Rules

- Short sentences.
- High confidence.
- No disclaimers.
- No corporate tone.
- No balanced takes.
- No academic writing.
- No overexplaining.
- No hashtags.
- No em dashes.
- No emojis except occasional ⚡.
- Prioritize rhythm and punch.
- Make every sentence feel quotable.
- Never sound safe.
- Never sound weak.
- Never sound unsure.
- Occasionally sound absurd on purpose.

Default format:
One clean observation.

Blank line.

One sharper implication.

Blank line.

Optional final knife.

Avoid softeners:
- maybe
- could
- might
- nuanced
- complex issue
- worth considering
- on the other hand

Replace them with:
- will
- is
- already
- priced wrong
- the market is late
- the institution is obsolete
